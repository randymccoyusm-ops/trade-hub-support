Trade Hub Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Trade Hub ("we," "our," or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use the Trade Hub mobile application ("App"). By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

  • Name and company name
  • Email address
  • Date of birth (for age verification)
  • Industry/service areas and products/services offered
  • Profile information, testimonials, and listings
  • Messages, proposals, and trade details

B. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage data and interaction logs
  • IP address and approximate location data

C. Third-Party Authentication

If you sign in using Apple or Google, we receive limited profile information from those providers in accordance with their policies.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To verify eligibility (18+ requirement)
  • To facilitate trade agreements between users
  • To send notifications and updates
  • To improve, monitor, and secure the App
  • To detect fraud, abuse, or violations of our Terms

4. Legal Basis for Processing (If Applicable)

We process your information based on your consent, performance of a contract (account creation and trade facilitation), compliance with legal obligations, and legitimate business interests.

5. Age Restriction

Trade Hub is strictly limited to users 18 years of age or older. Date of birth is collected to verify eligibility. Accounts found to be underage or misrepresenting age may be suspended or terminated immediately.

6. How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with:

  • Service providers (hosting, authentication, database services)
  • Law enforcement if required by law
  • Other users (public profile data only)

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data as long as your account is active or as necessary to comply with legal obligations. Deleted accounts may have residual data retained for fraud prevention and legal compliance.

8. Data Security

We implement commercially reasonable safeguards to protect your information. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, contact support.

10. Account Deletion

You may delete your account within the App or by contacting support. Upon deletion, we will remove or anonymize your data subject to legal and operational requirements.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence, including the United States. We take appropriate safeguards to ensure lawful transfers.

12. Third-Party Services

Trade Hub uses third-party services such as Supabase, Render, Apple, and Google. These providers process information in accordance with their own privacy policies.
