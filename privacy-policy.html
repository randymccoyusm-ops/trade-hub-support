We may collect the following information when you use Trade Hub:

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

3. Age Restriction

Trade Hub is restricted to individuals 18 years or older. Date of birth is collected solely to verify eligibility. Accounts found to misrepresent age may be suspended or terminated.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Limited information may be shared with service providers necessary to operate the app (such as hosting and authentication providers). Public profile information may be visible to other users.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

6. Your Rights

You may update your profile information within the app. To request account deletion, contact support.

7. Account Deletion

You may delete your account within the app settings or by emailing support. Upon deletion, your account information will be removed in accordance with applicable laws.

8. Third-Party Services

Trade Hub may use third-party services such as Supabase, Render, and authentication providers (Apple and Google). These providers may process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.
